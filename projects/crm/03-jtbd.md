## Job Name:
Know what to do next in sales

## Core Job Statement:
When I start my workday, I want to know exactly who to contact and what to say so I can close more deals without thinking about the system.

## Desired Outcomes:
- Clear prioritized actions
- No data entry
- No searching for information
- Immediate execution

## Emotional/Social Dimension:
- Feel in control
- Avoid overwhelm
- Avoid admin frustration

## Current Frictions:
- Too many screens
- Manual updates
- Static dashboards
- Forgotten follow-ups

## Existing Alternatives:
- Salesforce
- HubSpot
- Pipedrive
- Spreadsheets

## Importance (1–5):
5

## Satisfaction (1–5):
2
