# Vision Statement – Conversational CRM

CRM should not be software you manage.
It should be a system that manages your next best action.

We are building a conversation-first CRM that eliminates screens, dashboards, and manual updates — and simply tells you what to do to close more deals.

## Target Groups
- Sales reps
- Startup founders
- Customer success managers

## Needs
- No dashboards
- No manual updates
- Clear next action
- Fast input/output

## Features
- Conversational interface (chat-first)
- “What should I do now?” command
- Automatic email & call logging
- Auto-updated pipeline
- One-click task execution
- Voice input

## Business goals
- Reduce CRM fatigue
- Increase revenue per rep
- Minimize onboarding time

## Differentiators
- No traditional screens
- No analytics dashboards
- No manual pipeline management
- Action-only interface
