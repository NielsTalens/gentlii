## Mission
Replace complex CRM software with a conversational system that tells you exactly what to do next.

## Core Strategic Pillars
- Conversation over navigation
- Actions over dashboards
- AI-driven prioritization
- Zero manual data entry

## Target Customer
- Sales teams (SMB–mid market)
- Founders doing sales
- Account executives overwhelmed by CRM admin

## Value Proposition
“A CRM that talks to you and tells you what to do.”

## Success Metrics
- 50% reduction in CRM time spent
- 80% actions executed via chat
- +20% increase in follow-up rate
- Daily active usage >70%

## Long-term Vision
CRM becomes an invisible execution layer powered by conversation.
